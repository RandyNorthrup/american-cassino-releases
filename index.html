Features

Everything you need for the classic card game

🃏

Full Rule Set

Capture, trail, build, and call — complete American Cassino rules with multi-card build support.

🤖

Smart AI

Three difficulty tiers: Easy, Medium, and Hard. Hard mode uses opening books and transposition tables.

🌐

Online Multiplayer

Host or join games via relay server. Quick match, friend invites, and ranked play with Elo ratings.

🏆

Leaderboards

Track your best scores locally and compete in ranked matches for Elo rating glory.

🎨

Themes

Classic parchment or modern blue cards. Green felt or dark wood playmats. Your table, your style.

♿

Accessible

Full keyboard navigation, reduced motion, high contrast, and enlarged text options.

System Requirements

Windows

Windows 10 or later (64-bit). ~120 MB disk space. No additional dependencies needed.

macOS

macOS 12 Monterey or later. Universal binary (Intel + Apple Silicon). ~150 MB disk space.

Linux

Ubuntu 22.04+ or Debian-based. Qt 6 runtime libraries. ~100 MB disk space.
